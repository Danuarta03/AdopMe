Rules Donation

Here are some rules for donation:
1. Give Sincerely: Pet donation is a voluntary act. All contributions should come from the heart. There is no pressure or obligation.
2. Pet Condition: Please make sure the pet you wish to donate is in good health. We only accept animals that are healthy and do not have infectious diseases.
3. Complete Information: Include complete information about the pet you wish to donate. This includes the breed of animal, age, gender, vaccination history, and other important details.
4. Clean and Groomed: The pet should be clean and well-groomed. Bathe and feed it well before shipping.
5. Animal Delivery: The delivery of the animal can be done in a way that is most convenient for the receiving party. It can be through in-person meeting or delivery with the delivery cost being borne by the receiving party.
6. Consider the New Environment: Make sure that your pet will be surrendered to a safe and friendly environment for them. Ask the prospective recipient about their home and family conditions.
7. Long-term Commitment: Ensure that the potential recipient has a long-term commitment to caring for the pet. Pets are a long-term responsibility.
Always be careful and think carefully before handing over your pet to someone else. Above all, make sure the welfare of the animal is the top priority.
